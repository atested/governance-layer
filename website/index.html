Every action. Evaluated. Recorded. Provable.

Atested is governance infrastructure for AI operations. Every action your AI agents take is evaluated against policy, signed into an immutable record, and available as auditable proof.

See pricing Get started on GitHub

Policy evaluation

Every action is evaluated against your policy before execution. You define the rules. The governance layer enforces them consistently, across every tool call, every session, every operator. No action bypasses evaluation.

Signed records

Every decision is cryptographically signed into an immutable chain. Each record includes the policy decision, the action context, and a hash linking it to the previous record. Tampering with any record breaks the chain.

Proof packets

Generate verifiable attestation artifacts for any audit or review. A proof packet contains the decision records, the policy that produced them, and cryptographic evidence that nothing has been altered. Hand it to compliance, to a customer, or to your future self.

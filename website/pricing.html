How pricing works

Install and start your trial

Install Atested from GitHub and start your 30-day trial with full functionality. Every feature, every tool, every integration. No credit card required.

Your system attests its own usage

At trial end, your own Atested system generates an attestation artifact showing your usage: unique users, governed operations, and tool categories. This is the same cryptographically signed attestation you'd show an auditor. It determines your recommended tier.

Purchase your license

Purchase a license for your recommended tier. Your license key activates in your Atested server, and your governance records reflect your licensed status from that moment forward.

Renewal adjusts to actual usage

At renewal, your attestation artifact determines your next tier — up or down based on actual usage. If your team grew, you move up. If it shrank, you move down. The system records truth.
